Import Products from CSV

üìã CSV Format: Your file should have headers and these columns:
  • name - Product name (required)
  • opening_stock - Initial stock quantity (required, immutable)
  • selling_price - Selling price in UGX (required)
  • cost_price - Cost price in UGX (required)
  • reorder_level - Reorder level (optional, default: 10)

Example:

name,opening_stock,selling_price,cost_price,reorder_level
Coca Cola 500ml,100,2000,1500,20
Fanta Orange 500ml,80,2000,1500,20
